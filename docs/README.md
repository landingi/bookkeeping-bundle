# Bookkeeping logic

## Company
### Poland (Case 1.1)

* VAT NP - It is not subject to VAT
* MOSS not applicable

### The World (Case 1.3)

## Physical Person
### Poland (Case 2.1)

* VAT 23%
* MOSS not applicable

### European Union (Case 2.2)

* VAT rate from the country person is from
* MOSS is applicable

### The World (Case 2.3)

* VAT NP - It is not subject to VAT
* MOSS not applicable
